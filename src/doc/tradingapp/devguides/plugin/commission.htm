Commission derived classes are used in back-testing to simulate the effect of commission on trading systems. 

Besides the plug-in API, a commission plug-in must implement the Commission interface.

The method getValue is called by the platform and it must return the commission value calculated based on the parameters passed to it such as shares, price. Other relevant parameters may be added in the future. The returned value is the commission absolute value.
