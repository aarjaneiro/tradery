Right-clicking on objects in the plug-in tree will bring up a contextual menu that has commands relevant to the selected object(s). These commands also depend on what type of actions are supported by the plug-in or plug-in configuration. For instance, some plug-ins may allow creation of new configurations or editing and deleting of existing configurations, while others may not.

Properties Bar

The properties bar shows the properties (such as name, description and id) of the currently selected object, be it a plug-in type, plug-in instance or plug-in configuration.
